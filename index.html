About Me

My name is Tyler and I am a third year at UC San Diego where I am pursuing a degree in Mathematics-Computer Science. I was born and raised in Sacramento, California where I attended C.K. McClatchy highschool where I played baseball and wrestled for all four years. Now that I am in San Diego I love going to Padres games and taking trips to the beach every few days!


My Journey in Software

When entering college at UC San Diego I had no real concept of what software engineering really was. In fact, most things relating to computers were somewhat of a mystery to me. On a whim, I decided to take an intro to programming class at the end of my freshman year and I found that I really enjoyed it. From then on I was hooked, I knew that software was what I wanted to do. I decided to pursue a degree in Mathematics-Computer Science the next quarter and now after two years, and tons of computer science classes, I'm now getting ready to graduate this Spring.
