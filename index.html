About Me

McClatchy highschool. There I would play baseball and wrestle for all four years. Now that I am in San Diego I love going to Padres games and taking a trip down to the beach every few days!

My Journey in Software

When entering college at UC San Diego I had no real concept of what software engineering really way. In fact, most things relating to computers were somewhat of a mystery to me. On a whim, I decided to take an intro to programming class at the end of my freshman year and I found that I really enjoyed it. From then on I was hooked, I knew that software was what I wanted to do. I decided to pursue a Mathematics-Computer Science degree the next quarter after that intro class and now two years later, and sevral more computer science classes later, I'm now getting ready to graduate this Spring.
